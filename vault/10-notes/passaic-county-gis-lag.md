# Passaic County GIS lags deed transfers ~3 weeks

(Example note — replace with your own.) Recent ownership/sale data on the
county GIS portal can be ~3 weeks stale. For any sale or transfer within the
last 60 days, verify against the County Clerk's record search before relying
on GIS.

**Why it matters:** stale ownership on the subject or a comp triggers
revision requests and USPAP credibility issues.
